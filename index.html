About US

About BFL Floral Blessing

BFL Floral Blessing, founded by Mrs. Serene Pang, is a florist with a boutique concept that aims to provide consulting services and provide fresh flowers located in the heart of Hong Kong. Inspired by the love of nature and the beauty of the appearance of flowers, we believe we can provide a solution to your needs to express love and beauty with flowers.

Our passionate florist team creates designs with surprising touches to bring out the diversity of flowers in your vocabulary and character. We offer tailor made flower bouquets and floral gifts for every occasion. Every piece we make is highly personalized and the flowers we use are of the highest quality and freshest available. We also offer special seasonal bouquets using the best seasonal materials.
